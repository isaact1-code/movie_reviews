Jumanji (1995)

Description: A thrilling adventure starring Robin Williams, Jumanji (1995) is a beloved classic that centers around a magical board game that brings the jungle to life in dangerous and exciting ways.

Plot: Two kids find a magical board game that brings wild animals and dangerous adventures into the real world, forcing them to finish the game or face disastrous consequences.

Star Rating: ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ 3.5

Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle (2017)

Description: The modern reboot of Jumanji introduces a new twist, where four high school students are sucked into a video game and must play as their avatars to survive the jungle and escape the game.

Plot: Four students discover an old video game console and are transformed into their chosen avatars, each with unique skills, and must work together to escape a deadly jungle.

Star Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.5

Jumanji: The Next Level (2019)

Description: The gang is back for another adventure in the world of Jumanji, but things have changed. They will have to navigate new terrains and face more dangerous obstacles to survive.

Plot: As they return to rescue one of their own, the players will have to brave parts unknown from arid deserts to snowy mountains, with new challenges at every turn.

Color Scheme

This website uses a jungle-inspired color palette featuring deep greens, teal, and light accents. The colors reflect the adventurous and nature-themed concept of the Jumanji movies.

Typography

We use Montserrat for headings to give a modern, bold look and Lora for body text, offering a traditional, easy-to-read serif font that balances style and readability.

Concept

The concept draws from the adventurous and exciting themes of the Jumanji movies. The clean layout, card-style presentation, and hover effects bring an interactive, game-like experience to the viewer.

Layout

The design uses a grid-based layout to ensure responsive behavior and clear separation of content. Each section is boxed and padded to make the site visually appealing and easy to navigate.
